Tribal Court

The goal of the Kootenai Tribal Court is to provide a Tribal Court System that supports the Kootenai Tribe, tribal members and families while respecting the inherent tribal sovereignty, reserved rights, religious, cultural traditional practices while encouraging individual responsibility for life choices, and asking respect for one another and the laws and codes of the Kootenai Tribe. The Tribal Court is available twice a month.
