Law Enforcement

The Kootenai Tribe of Idaho Police Department enforces Kootenai Tribe and federal laws on the Kootenai Indian Reservation. The officers are responsible for a wide variety of duties involving maintaining the peace, and protecting life and property in the community.

The Department works with law enforcement officials at the federal, state, county, and provincial levels to promote cooperative and collaborative law enforcement.
