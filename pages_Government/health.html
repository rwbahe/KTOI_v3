Health Clinic

Opened in 1993, the Tribal Health Clinic offers on-site physician service, as well as RN evaluations and immunizations. Counseling services are offered weekly and a nurse practioner is available quarterly for women's health exams. The clinic's Contract Health Services provides funding for dental, optical, and specialist visits for Tribal Members.

Outreach and education in areas such as diabetes, immunization, smoking prevention, and substance abuse are also available.
